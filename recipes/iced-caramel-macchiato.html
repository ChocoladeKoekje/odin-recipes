Iced Caramel Macchiato

Description

A starbucks copycat recipe to make this iced drink at home

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vanilla syrup
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 capsules nespresso espresso (or 2 espresso shots)
  • 1 tablespoon caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Pour vanilla syrup, milk, then ice into a cup. Top with espresso shots.Use 2 nespresso espresso pods or 2 shots from an espresso machine
  2. Add caramel drizzle to the top of the drink.To add caramel just like starbucks, drizzle in crosshatch pattern, layering the caramel drizzle 7 times vertically across the surface of the drink, 7 times horizontally, then 2 circles around the edge of the cup.
