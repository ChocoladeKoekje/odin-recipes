Ice Coffee

Description

Make a perfectly sweet and delicious iced coffee at hpme with easy step by step instructions. prep time is 3 min, total time is 3 min, course is drinks and it yields 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Ice
  • 1 cup of strong coffee
  • 2 tablespoons simple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons half & half

Instructions

  1. pour strong, cooled coffee over ice.Use cooled down or cold coffee. A good balance is half strong coffee and half ice.
  2. stir in simple syrup.Add more or less, depending on how sweet you like your iced coffee.
  3. add half & halfAdd more or less, depending on how creamy you like your iced coffee.
